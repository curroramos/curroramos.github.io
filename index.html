I have worked as a research intern at the Higher Technical School of Engineering (ETSI) in Seville. I have also worked as a research intern at the Advanced Centre for Aerospace Technologies (CATEC) in Seville. I am currently doing research on the use of state-of-the-art Deep Learning models for Object Detection applied in Robotics in different tasks related to air surveillance and security. My goal is to transform the world and make it a better place by solving real problems with new approaches using the latest techniques.

I am seaking out new opportunities to broaden my knowledge and develop my professional career. If you think I would be a good fit for your lab or company, feel free to reach out to me.

Research interest

I am highly interested in the research of Deep Learning and Machine Learning applied to Control and Robotics, and I am especially interested in research projects that can help create a better future. My main interest is Computer Vision and Time Series processing applied to Robotics and Control systems .
